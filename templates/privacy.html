Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

database

Data Storage

We do not maintain a mirrored database. All creator information persists solely within the Google Sheet you connect. Influenze Relay acts as a transient processor, reading data only at the moment of outreach execution.

share_off

Zero External Sharing

Your campaign data is your competitive advantage. We strictly adhere to a zero-sharing policy. No data is sold, anonymized for trends, or transmitted to third-party analytics vendors.

encrypted

Direct Communication

Messages are sent end-to-end encrypted via WhatsApp's standard protocol. Influenze Relay does not and cannot intercept the content of your messages once they are dispatched.

visibility_off

No Reply Tracking

To ensure total privacy of negotiation details, the system ignores incoming messages. All replies must be managed manually on your device. We do not store or process creator responses.
