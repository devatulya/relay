Help Center

Answers to common questions about your outreach tools.

How do I start outreach? expand_more
  1. Navigate to the main dashboard.
  2. Ensure the dedicated browser window for WhatsApp Web is open and logged in.
  3. Input your campaign details (Brand Name, Niche, Budget, Deliverables, etc.).
  4. Click "Launch Automation".
  5. The system will direct you to a Preview Stage, where you can verify matching creators and calculated payouts.
  6. Review the list, optionally uncheck any creators you want to skip, and confirm the dispatch.
What if WhatsApp is not connected? expand_more
The system detects session status automatically. If disconnected, it will present a QR Code on the screen. Scan this with your mobile device (WhatsApp > Linked Devices) to re-authenticate properly before launching campaigns.
What if a creator was already contacted? expand_more
We maintain a "Last Contacted" timestamp in your Google Sheet. The system comprehensively filters out any creator contacted within the mandatory cooldown period (default: 30 days) ensuring we strictly prevent spamming or burning bridges.
How is the creator payout calculated? expand_more
The payout calculation relies on two variables: your specified campaign budget, and the creator's requested rate. The system computes a Base Price by taking the minimum of those two values. The Final Total Payout offered is that Base Price multiplied by the number of deliverables.
What happens if a product is non-retainable? expand_more
During setup, you specify if the product is retainable. If you select "No", the system automatically injects a specific waiver text block into the message template, making it abundantly clear up-front that the creator must securely return the product after filming.
Can I remove creators from the outreach list before sending? expand_more
Yes! Before any message is fired, you enter the Preview Stage interface. This view plots all matched outreach targets and allows you to manually uncheck out-of-scope creators from the dispatch queue.
Who handles replies? expand_more
Influenze Relay is a one-way outreach tool intentionally. It does not scrape or read incoming messages for your privacy and security. It is your team's responsibility to monitor the active WhatsApp account.
